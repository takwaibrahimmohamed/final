UMI F&B GROUP HOLDINGS SDN BHD

Pioneering Excellence: At the heart of UMI lies a relentless pursuit of perfection. Our journey, enriched with passion and precision, has seen us metamorphose into a premier asset management firm, dedicated exclusively to the vibrant world of F&B.
